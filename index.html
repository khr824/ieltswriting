IELTS Writing Toolkit

Task 1: Trends

Upward: skyrocketed, surged, climbed gradually.
Downward: plummeted, dipped, slumped.
Stability: remained constant, leveled off.

Tip: Never give your opinion in Task 1! Only report the data.

Task 2: Connectors

Contrast: Conversely, Nevertheless, Despite this.
Addition: Furthermore, Moreover, In addition.
Conclusion: To encapsulate, In light of these facts.

Tip: Use the 'PEEL' method for paragraphs (Point, Evidence, Explain, Link).
